Network Simulation Healthcare

Simulation Healthcare Education provides realistic and immersive training scenarios, improving clinical skills and decision-making. It allows learners to practice and refine their techniques without the risk of harming real patients.

about us

NHS WORKGROUP


The NSH Flanders (Network Simulation Healthcare) is a group of multiple schools with a core team of four schools: EhB, Vives, UCLL and KDG. We work together as a united community to give the opportunity to nurses and professionals to develop more skills if needed in certain situations. With our simulation dolls it's like you're working in real circumstances.

WHAT ARE OUR GOALS?


  • Uniting simulation education in Flemish colleges,across associations
  • Share and discuss our best practices
  • Coming out stronger as one community
  • Publish research as one community
